OpenTBL

OpenTBL is a learning platform designed specifically for Team-based Learning (TBL) Pedagogy. Users can create peer evaluations and readiness assurance tests in minutes and distribute them among their classrooms with the click of a button.

As the sole designer at Sixth Edge, I was involved in every step of the product process. From initial requirement gathering to sketching to branding and then implementation.

Process, process, process

In addition to leading the Product Design team, I directed the 2017 redesign of Trunk Club’s marketing site, focusing on the at-home trunk business while reshaping a more approachable brand.
