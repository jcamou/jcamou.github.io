Team-Based Learning Exercise Builder

OpenTBL

After adding a Team-based Learning (TBL) feature to ThinkSpace, one of our learning platforms, we noticed an unusual increase in tickets regarding the exercise builder. ThinkSpace housed a variety of discipline-specific tools that could be added to any exercise, so complexity wasn’t unfamiliar to its users. However, given that this tool was the first one to add team functionality to the platform compounded with the number of tickets coming in, we decided to take a closer look at the problem.

The result was OpenTBL, a learning platform designed specifically for TBL, containing a simplified exercise builder and support articles.

Problem

Complexity with little support

The majority of the problems stemmed from the number of disciplines and learning tools ThinkSpace catered to. This is a strength of the platform when building more complicated exercises, but can be brutal for someone building something as simple as a Peer Evaluation. Too many options, too many steps, and too many opportunities to get lost.
