ThinkSpace

ThinkSpace is a learning platform that focuses on critical thinking and pedagogy. Instructors can create complex cases by breaking each learning objective into an individual step. ThinkSpace offers discipline specific tools to make each case more effective and improve student learning.

One example of those tools is ThinkSpace + Diagnostics used by Veterinary Medicine. In each case, Veterinary students are taken through the same process as a professional veterinary to diagnose and treat sick animals.
